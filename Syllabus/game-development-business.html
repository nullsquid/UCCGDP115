COURSE DESCRIPTION: This course covers many aspects related to the business of game development. Students learn about legal issues, publishing, marketing, entrepreneurship, and current topics in the game industry. Some concepts covered include intellectual property rights, branding, public relations, contract negotiations, choosing a business entity, and writing business and marketing plans. This course will help students gain a broader understanding of the game development industry.

 

COURSE LEARNING OUTCOMES:

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

1. Understand the role of a publisher in game development

2. Discuss how a business organizes itself legally and the process of incorporation

3. Explain the importance of intellectual property, copyrights, trade secrets, trademarks, and patents in the game industry

4. Understand the role of game industry contracts and strategies for negotiating them

5. Discuss fundamentals of marketing, branding, and public relations as it relates to game companies

6. Understand the role of the business plan, marketing plan, and marketing campaign

7. Locate, discern, and effectively use information to solve issues and/or problems in game design and development
